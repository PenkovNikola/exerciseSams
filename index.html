About Baswi

The Bay area womens sportrs os a public benefit nonptofit corporation with a mission to create programs and partnerships through which women athletes bring health hope and wholeness to our comunity. Founded in 2005 by Olymic and World cup soccer stars Julie Foudy and Marlene Bjornsrud, former general menager of the San Jose Cyber Womans proffesional soccer team, Bawsi provides a neaningful path for women athletes to become more visible and valued part of the bay area culture.
